White Dwarf

White dwarfs ares a stellar core remnants composed mostly of electron-degenerate matter. They are thought to be the fianl evolutionary state of a star whose mass is not high enough to become a neutron star or a black hole.

Red Dwarf

A red dwarf is the smallest kind of star on the main sequence. It's luminosity and temperature is a percentage of that of our Sun. They may davelop into red giants or white dwarfs depending on their mass.

The Sun

Our sun is the star in the middle of our solar system. About 4 to 7 billion years from now the sun will transform into a red giant. After that it will become a cooling star, a white dwarf.

Red Giant

Reg giants are luminous giant stars that have evolved from main sequence stars with the correct mass. It will usually produce a planetary nebula and become a white dwarf at the end of its life.
